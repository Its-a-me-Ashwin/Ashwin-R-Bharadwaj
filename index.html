About

I graduated from PES University, Bangalore, India from 2017 - 2021, with a major in Computer Science and Engineering and a specialization in Algorithms and Computing Models. My major undergraduate research project was done under the guidance of Dr. Natarajan S, Srinivas KS, and Srikanth HR. During my undergraduate studies, I interned a the photonics lab at PES University, Microsoft Innovation Lab, and Cisco Systems. Since graduation, I have been working at Cisco Systems as a backend software developer.

My hobbies include robotics and 3D printing and building various projects. I have built various robotic arms, many different varieties of E-bikes, and 3D-printed drones/boats and aircraft. I also enjoy the outdoors and especially cycling around the city.
