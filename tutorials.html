Video Transmitter: Decent quality; compatible with 5.8GHz video receivers. It works well in my testing.
DSMX Receiver (7Ch): Not tested due to lack of a DSMX transmitter. The manufacturer made a design oversight: installing the receiver requires sharply bending the antennas, potentially exceeding their minimum bending radius and leading to premature failure. This could have been addressed with inexpensive stand-offs or longer connectors.

D-Big (Combat Robot)

Feb 2024

D-Big is a Melty Brain robot. Unlike a traditional bot, D-Big spins in place, to increase the kinetic energy.
To control the direction of the bot, we modulate the speed of the motors based on the angle of the bot. We utilize an IMU (Inertial Measurement Unit) along with a magnetometer to measure the current heading of the bot. The reference heading is calculated with respect to the original direction of the bot during startup.
